It was a dark and stormy night...
And all through the house...

Nothing was stirring; not even a mouse

Then, the wind blew hard outside the window

It blew the window open and rain poured inside. Creating a puddle in the kitchen

The puddle reveals a dip in the kitchen floor.
"Damn, damn, damn!" she screamed as the floor began to sink in.

The water began to morph into an impossibly large whirlpool.

The whirlpool engulfed the entire kitchen and swallowed the kids and dog!

More screaming ensued as she tried to grasp what was actually happening. How? Why her?
